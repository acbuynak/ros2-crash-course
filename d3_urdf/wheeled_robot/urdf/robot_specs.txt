Here are the specifications for the simple mobile robot:

	Links:
	1. One red cylinder with a radius of 0.05 meters and height of 0.02 meters for the base
	2. One green cube with side length of 0.05 meters for the head
	3. Two blue cylinders with a radius of 0.03 meters and height of 0.01 meters for the wheels

	Joints:
	1. A fixed joint that attaches the head to the base link
	2. Two revolute joints that:
		a. attach the wheels to the base link
		b. rotate around the z-axis
		c. have a rotation limit of +/- 2pi radians
